## Intoduction

wxMP3val is a free front-end for the MP3val. Dedicated to MP3 files, wxMP3val lets you scan your music collection for corrupt files, incomplete downloads, and missing tags in an attempt to fix them.

Using it is pretty simple. Once finished adding files or the folders that contain your MP3 tracks to its main window (supports drag-n-drop), hit the ‘Scan’ button, if it finds errors, then those tracks will be marked in Red. For fixing the tracks, click on the ‘Repair’ button, that’s it!

![Screenshot](https://a.fsdn.com/con/app/proj/wxmp3val/screenshots/Main.png/max/max/1)

## Features

* Supports batch file (MP3) scanning and repairing;
* Once scanned, it shows details such as the audio layer version, meta-tag version, if the file was encoded using CBR (constant bit rate) and the condition ("OK" means you’re good to go, "PROBLEM" means, oh you know :D);
* "MP3val" can fix some of those playback/encode issues but it's recommended that you keep a backup of the original files first, just in case.
